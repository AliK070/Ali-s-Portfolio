Discriminant Calculator:

A simple tool for finding solutions to quadratic equations using the formula b²-4ac. This is know as the discriminant. Made with C# for easy use and the .NET Framework, it features:

  • Updated Display: Shows your entered numbers.

  • Custom Settings: Change themes and enable graphs and other features.

  • Basic Save Function: Remembers your settings.
